Here is detailed what each reconstructed folder contains. For convenience, only the most important files (for this project) are explicitly listed. You can refer to the documentation of the specific tool for more informations.

.
├── pipeline_config.env: Config file used to store the parameters that run the reconstruction.
├── raw/: Raw input volumes (renamed to be standard across subjects: T1.nii.gz, T2.nii.gz, DTI.nii.gz)
│   └── T1.nii.gz
├── fastsurfer/: Output of the volumetric stream in the fastsurfer tool. Mainly used to generate finer atlases.
│   └── mri/
│       ├── cerebellum.CerebNet.nii.gz: Volumetric labelling of cerebellum performed using the CerebNet architecture.
│       └── hypothalamus.HypVINN.nii.gz: Volumetric labels of the hypothalamus performed using the HypVINN architecture.
├── hippunfold/: Output of the hippunfold tool, used to extract and lable hippocampal surfaces.
│   ├── LABELS.txt: Labels used in the atlases generated by the tool.
│   ├── anat/:
│   │   ├── sub-subject_hemi-L_space-T1w_desc-subfields_atlas-multihist7_dseg.nii.gz: Volumetric labels of the left hippocampus in subject space.
│   │   └── sub-subject_hemi-R_space-T1w_desc-subfields_atlas-multihist7_dseg.nii.gz: Volumetric labels of the left hippocampus in subject space.
│   └── surf/
│       ├── sub-subject_hemi-L_space-T1w_den-0p5mm_label-dentate_midthickness.surf.gii: Left midthickness dentate surface.
│       ├── sub-subject_hemi-L_space-T1w_den-0p5mm_label-dentate_surfarea.shape.gii: Per vertex area associate to left dentate surface.
│       ├── sub-subject_hemi-L_space-T1w_den-0p5mm_label-hipp_atlas-multihist7_subfields.label.gii: Per vertex labels associate to left hippocampal surface.
│       ├── sub-subject_hemi-L_space-T1w_den-0p5mm_label-hipp_midthickness.surf.gii: Left midthickness hippocampal surface.
│       ├── sub-subject_hemi-L_space-T1w_den-0p5mm_label-hipp_surfarea.shape.gii: Per vertex area associate to left hippocampal surface.
│       ├── sub-subject_hemi-L_space-T1w_den-0p5mm_label-hipp_thickness.shape.gii: Per vertex thickness associate to left hippocampal surface.
│       ├── sub-subject_hemi-R_space-T1w_den-0p5mm_label-dentate_midthickness.surf.gii: Right midthickness dentate surface.
│       ├── sub-subject_hemi-R_space-T1w_den-0p5mm_label-dentate_surfarea.shape.gii: Per vertex area associate to right dentate surface.
│       ├── sub-subject_hemi-R_space-T1w_den-0p5mm_label-hipp_atlas-multihist7_subfields.label.gii: Per vertex labels associate to right hippocampal surface.
│       ├── sub-subject_hemi-R_space-T1w_den-0p5mm_label-hipp_midthickness.surf.gii: Right midthickness hippocampal surface.
│       ├── sub-subject_hemi-R_space-T1w_den-0p5mm_label-hipp_surfarea.shape.gii: Per vertex area associate to right hippocampal surface.
│       └── sub-subject_hemi-R_space-T1w_den-0p5mm_label-hipp_thickness.shape.gii: Per vertex thickness associate to right hippocampal surface.
├── freesurfer/: Output of the freesurfer recon-all stream, MNE bem surfaces reconstruction, Schaefer atlases registrations, Freesurfer subcortical streams (hippoamydala, thalamus, brainstem).
│   ├── FreeSurferColorLUT.txt: Freesurfer LUT.
│   ├── Schaefer_LUT/: LUT associated to Schaefer atlases.
│   ├── bem/: Output of mne bem surface reconstruction.
│   ├── mri/
│   │   ├── ThalamicNuclei.mgz: Thalamic stream output.
│   │   ├── aseg.mgz: Standard subcortical segmentation.
│   │   ├── brainstemSsLabels.mgz: Brainstem stream output.
│   │   ├── lh.hippoAmygLabels.mgz: Left hippoamygdala stream output.
│   │   ├── rh.hippoAmygLabels.mgz: Right hippoamygdala stream output.
│   │   └── schaefer*_aparc+aseg.mgz: Volumetric Schaefer atlas registered to the subject.
│   └── surf/
│       ├── lh.area: Per vertex area associated to left cortical surface.
│       ├── lh.pial: Left cortical pial surface.
│       ├── lh.thickness: Per vertex thickness associated to left cortical surface.
│       ├── lh.volume: Per vertex volume associated to left cortical surface.
│       ├── lh.white: Left cortical white surface.
│       ├── rh.area: Per vertex area associated to right cortical surface.
│       ├── rh.pial: Right cortical pial surface.
│       ├── rh.thickness: Per vertex thickness associated to right cortical surface.
│       ├── rh.volume: Per vertex volume associated to right cortical surface.
│       └── rh.white: Right cortical white surface.
├── simnibs_charm/: Output of SimNibs charm stream.
│   ├── converted/: Surfaces extracted from simnibs generated tetrahedral mesh (subject.msh).
│   ├── final_tissues.nii.gz: Tissues volumetric label field.
│   ├── final_tissues_LUT.txt: LUT associated to above label field.
│   └── subject.msh: Tetrahedral mesh generated from the above tissue label field.
├── fsl_first/: Output of FSL First tool.
│   ├── T1.nii.gz: Bias field corrected T1 file (using ANTs).
│   ├── FSL-BrStem_first.vtk: Brainstem surface.
│   ├── FSL-L_Accu_first.vtk: Left accumbens surface.
│   ├── FSL-L_Amyg_first.vtk: Left amygdala surface.
│   ├── FSL-L_Caud_first.vtk: Left caudate surface.
│   ├── FSL-L_Hipp_first.vtk: Left hippocampus surface.
│   ├── FSL-L_Pall_first.vtk: Left pallidum surface.
│   ├── FSL-L_Puta_first.vtk: Left putamen surface.
│   ├── FSL-L_Thal_first.vtk: Left thalamus surface.
│   ├── FSL-R_Accu_first.vtk: Right accumbens surface.
│   ├── FSL-R_Amyg_first.vtk: Right amygdala surface.
│   ├── FSL-R_Caud_first.vtk: Right caudate surface.
│   ├── FSL-R_Hipp_first.vtk: Right hippocampus surface.
│   ├── FSL-R_Pall_first.vtk: Right pallidum surface.
│   ├── FSL-R_Puta_first.vtk: Right putamen surface.
│   └── FSL-R_Thal_first.vtk: Right thalamus surface.
├── synthstrip/: Output of synthstrip, a brain extraction tool.
├── cerebellum/: Output of ANTs registration of subject to cerebellum atlas.
│   ├── LABELS.csv: Labels used in cerebellum atlases.
│   ├── affine_*.vtk: Affine transformed (to subject space) cerebellar surfaces.
│   ├── affine_*.nii.gz: Affine transformed (to subject space) cerebellar volumes.
│   ├── nonlinear_*.vtk: Nonlinearly transformed (to subject space) cerebellar surfaces.
│   ├── nonlinear_*.nii.gz: Nonlinearly transformed (to subject space) cerebellar volumes.
│   └── transform_files/: Computed transformation files, from Subject to Template.
├── surfaces/: Surface files from various tools converted to world space and aggregated here.
│   ├── cereb_gray.stl: Pial cerebellar surface (nonlinearly transformed from template).
│   ├── cereb_white.stl: White cerebellar surface (nonlinearly transformed from template).
│   ├── cereb_inner_raw.stl: Inner cerebellar surface (nonlinearly transformed from template).
│   ├── cereb_inner_raw.stl: Inner cerebellar surface, processed to become manifold (nonlinearly transformed from template).
│   ├── cereb_inner_original_labels.npy: Per vertex cerebellum labels associated to cereb_inner_raw.stl
│   ├── cereb_inner_thickness.npy: Per vertex cerebellum thickness associated to cereb_inner_raw.stl
│   ├── charm_*.stl: Surfaces extracted from SimNibs charm stream.
│   ├── first_*.stl: Surfaces extracted from FSL first stream.
│   ├── freesurfer_BEM_brain.stl: CSF-bran surface extracted with MNE watershed BEM.
│   ├── freesurfer_BEM_inner_skull.stl: Skull-CSF surface extracted with MNE watershed BEM.
│   ├── freesurfer_BEM_outer_skull.stl: Scalp-skull surface extracted with MNE watershed BEM.
│   ├── freesurfer_BEM_outer_skin.stl: Scalp surface extracted with MNE watershed BEM.
│   ├── freesurfer_lh_pial.stl: Freesurfer left pial cortical surface.
│   ├── freesurfer_lh_white.stl: Freesurfer left white cortical surface.
│   ├── freesurfer_lh_middle.stl: Freesurfer left midthickness cortical surface.
│   ├── freesurfer_lh_middle_original_labels_*.npy: Per vertex labels associated to freesurfer_lh_middle.stl
│   ├── freesurfer_lh_middle_thickness.npy: Per vertex thickness associated to freesurfer_lh_middle.stl
│   ├── freesurfer_lh_middle_volume.npy: Per vertex volumes associated to freesurfer_lh_middle.stl
│   ├── freesurfer_rh_pial.stl: Freesurfer right pial cortical surface.
│   ├── freesurfer_rh_white.stl: Freesurfer right white cortical surface.
│   ├── freesurfer_rh_middle.stl: Freesurfer right midthickness cortical surface.
│   ├── freesurfer_rh_middle_original_labels_*.npy: Per vertex labels associated to freesurfer_rh_middle.stl
│   ├── freesurfer_rh_middle_thickness.npy: Per vertex thickness associated to freesurfer_rh_middle.stl
│   ├── freesurfer_rh_middle_volume.npy: Per vertex volumes associated to freesurfer_rh_middle.stl
│   ├── hippunfold_L_dentate_middle.stl: Left dentate surface extracted using hippunfold.
│   ├── hippunfold_L_dentate_middle_original_labels.npy: Per vertex labels associated to hippunfold_L_dentate_middle.stl
│   ├── hippunfold_L_dentate_middle_thickness.npy: Per vertex thickness associated to hippunfold_L_dentate_middle.stl
│   ├── hippunfold_L_hipp_middle.stl: Left hippocampus surface extracted using hippunfold.
│   ├── hippunfold_L_hipp_middle_original_labels.npy: Per vertex labels associated to hippunfold_L_hipp_middle.stl
│   ├── hippunfold_L_hipp_middle_thickness.npy: Per vertex thickness associated to hippunfold_L_hipp_middle.stl
│   ├── hippunfold_R_dentate_middle.stl: Right dentate surface extracted using hippunfold.
│   ├── hippunfold_R_dentate_middle_original_labels.npy: Per vertex labels associated to hippunfold_R_dentate_middle.stl
│   ├── hippunfold_R_dentate_middle_thickness.npy: Per vertex thickness associated to hippunfold_R_dentate_middle.stl
│   ├── hippunfold_R_hipp_middle.stl: Right hippocampus surface extracted using hippunfold.
│   ├── hippunfold_R_hipp_middle_original_labels.npy: Per vertex labels associated to hippunfold_R_hipp_middle.stl
│   └── hippunfold_R_hipp_middle_thickness.npy: Per vertex thickness associated to hippunfold_R_hipp_middle.stl
├── atlas/: Contains volumetric atlases built on the input T1.
│   └── atlas*.nii.gz: Files of this form are label fields where subcortical regions are segmented into more than 100 labels. The file name indicate how many subregions are used to parcellate the cortex (e.g. atlas100.nii.gz contains 100 distinct labels for the cortex).
│   ├── atlas*_LUT.txt: LUT file associated to the volume above.
│   ├── atlas*_labels.txt: Label file associated to the volume above.
│   ├── atlas_aggregated.nii.gz: Simplified atlas that only divides main regions (26 in total, 24 subcortical and 1 for each cortical emisphere).
│   ├── atlas_aggregated_labels.txt: Label file for the volume above.
│   └── *_surf/: These folders are generated to keep track of vertices labels (associated to several, pre generated, surfaces) in the new atlases labels.
├── tissue_labels/: Label fields of tissues with electrical and acoustic properties.
│   └── sim4life_raw/: Label field generated with sim4life (optional, needs to be created manually)
│   ├── electrical/: Tissue labels with electrical properties.
│   │   ├── ITIS_conductivities.txt: Conductivities of tissues extracted from the ITIS database.
│   │   ├── sim4life.nii.gz: Label field extracted from sim4life (with some of the labels aggregated to have a conductivity for each tissue).
│   │   ├── sim4life_LUT.txt: LUT for the above volume.
│   │   ├── sim4life_labels.txt: Labels for the above volume.
│   │   ├── sim4life_conductivities.txt: Conductivities for each label in the sim4life label field.
│   │   ├── simnibs.nii.gz: Label field extracted using simnibs charm.
│   │   ├── simnibs_LUT.txt: LUT for the above volume.
│   │   ├── simnibs_labels.txt: Labels for the above volume.
│   │   └── simnibs_conductivities.txt: Conductivities (from simnibs website) for the above volume.
│   └── acoustic/: Tissue labels with acoustic properties.
│       ├── ITIS_attenuation_constant.txt: Attenuation constant of tissues extracted from the ITIS database.
│       ├── ITIS_density.txt: Density of tissues extracted from the ITIS database.
│       ├── ITIS_nonlinearity_parameter.txt: Nonlinearity parameter of tissues extracted from the ITIS database (nan is used when not available).
│       ├── ITIS_speed_of_sound.txt: Speed of sound of tissues extracted from the ITIS database.
│       ├── sim4life.nii.gz: Label field extracted from sim4life (with some of the labels aggregated to have a acoustic properties for each tissue).
│       ├── sim4life_LUT.txt: LUT for the above volume.
│       ├── sim4life_labels.txt: Labels for the above volume.
│       ├── sim4life_attenuation_constant.txt: Attenuation constant for each label in the sim4life label field.
│       ├── sim4life_density.txt: Density for each label in the sim4life label field.
│       ├── sim4life_nonlinearity_parameter.txt: Nonlinearity parameter for each label in the sim4life label field (nan is used when not available).
│       ├── sim4life_speed_of_sound.txt: Speed of sound for each label in the sim4life label field.
│       ├── simnibs.nii.gz: Label field extracted using simnibs charm.
│       ├── simnibs_LUT.txt: LUT for the above volume.
│       ├── simnibs_labels.txt: Labels for the above volume.
│       ├── simnibs_attenuation_constant.txt: Attenuation constant (from ITIS) for the simnibs volume.
│       ├── simnibs_density.txt: Density (from ITIS) for the simnibs volume.
│       ├── simnibs_nonlinearity_parameter.txt: Nonlinearity parameter (from ITIS) for the simnibs volume (nan is used when not available).
│       └── simnibs_speed_of_sound.txt: Speed of sound (from ITIS) for the simnibs volume.
├── scalp_landmarks/: Subject landmarks built on surfaces/charm_scalp.stl
│   ├── fiducials.json: RPA, LPA, NAS, IN positions.
│   └── outlines.npy: Right ear, left ear, and eyes outline, used to exclude electrodes.
├── electrodes/: Electrodes sampled accordingly to the 10-5 system, built on surfaces/charm_scalp.stl
│   ├── landmarks_10-5-full.csv: Electrodes positions sampled according to the 10-5 system.
│   └── selected_landmarks_10-5-full.json: Subset of electrodes that do not overlap with ears or eyes.
└── reconstruction_logs/: Logs extracted from the various reconstruction steps (one per folder).
